Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

1. How much does AgroGuard cost?

The price depends on the option you choose. We have direct purchase, monthly subscription, and rental systems. Contact us for full pricing and options suitable for you.

2. Does the robot need internet?

No! AgroGuard operates without internet. Its AI performs analysis directly on the robot, and alerts are sent via SMS.

3. What crops can the robot be used for?

Currently, AgroGuard is trained to detect pests and diseases in maize, rice, and vegetables. We can adapt it for other crops based on your needs.

4. Is the robot easy to maintain?

Yes! AgroGuard is designed with modular components, making maintenance straightforward. We also provide training on usage and upkeep.

5. How long does it take to scan a hectare?

It depends on the design of the farm, but on average, the robot takes about 2-3 hours to thoroughly scan one hectare.

6. Do you provide training?

Yes! We offer comprehensive training on how to operate and maintain the robot. Training is included in the purchase or subscription price.

7. Do you offer warranty services?

Yes! All robots come with a 1-year warranty covering hardware issues. Monthly subscriptions include maintenance.

8. Can I get a demo before purchasing?

Absolutely! We schedule free demonstrations for interested farmers. Contact us to arrange a visit to your farm or our school.
